MemForever is dedicated to help dementia patients

Dementia Exponential Growth


Every day, 250 people are diagnosed with dementia. Three in 10 people over the age of 85 and almost one in 10 people over 65 have dementia. Australian is an aging society, dementia is the second leading cause of death of Australians. MemForever is a web application dedicated to help dementia patients and their family.



Why using MemForever?


Dementia is a fatal disease not only that it is irreversible but every patient deteriorates at a different speed. How do we combat this disease when we don't know how sick we are?

MemForever is a platform dedicated to help dementia patients. Once registered, MemForever tracks and analyzes your conditions from your mood, cognition, BMI, and other health conditions in order to provide some insights into your progress.

What does each function do?


Mood tracking


Memforever helps you track your emotion everyday. This information can provide an important cue to you, your family, and your doctor to see how your moods have changed overtime.

Cognitive Game


Memforever provides a list of simple muliple choice questions aim to to keep your mind sharp. If you play the "game" on a daily basis, the score you received will be recorded to provide you some insight on your cognitive progression.



Health


MemForever helps to track your blood sugar, blood pressure and weight. When you enter your health data to Memforever on a daily basis, MemForever will analyse your health based on the data you provided, this information might be very useful for you, your family, and your doctor.


Data Analysis Report


By using MemForever platform on a daily basis, you would be able see a track record for your health condition, your game score and your mood. MemForever turns this track records into graphic report so that you can understand your well-being and spot any changes. General suggestions according to your health condition is also provided to help you adjust your life style accordingly.



To-do list


MemForever provides a simple to use to do list platform. You can add your daily to do tasks and once you have finished them, you can simply remove the task from the list.
