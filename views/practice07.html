Estimated number of Australians with dementia, 2018 to 2030

Dementia Exponential Growth



By 2030, about 550,000 Australians are expected to have dementia, and 42% of those aged 85 or older. By 2057, this number is expected to extend to about 1 million Australians.
Australian is an aging society, dementia is an urgent issue that should not be overlooked. MemForever is a website dedicated to help dementia patients and their family.
